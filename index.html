About Me

My name is Jorge Danilo, I am graduated in Systems Analysis and Development, I have more than 6 years of experience in the information technology profession. I have experience in the software area, including software development Web (FrontEnd, BackEnd), Mobile (Native / Hybrid), quality control and system testing. Still following good software development practices, project standards using SOLID concepts and clean architecture, ISO's standards have a theoretical and practical basis in quality control and quality assurance. I worked in several systems, among them Federal Highway Police, Regional Electoral Court of the state of Tocantins, Telephone Operators (VIVO), Pharmaceutical modules, mobile applications, financial systems. Advanced knowledge in Mobile Development and test automation of the most diverse platforms, including Web (Java, JSP, JSF, PHP) and Mobile. I currently work as Mobile Developer (Nativo / Flutter), using Java, Kotlin, Flutter, and the following technologies for Backend and Frontend (frameworks) Angular, Ionic 4, Cordova, RestFull Architecture, JSF, CDI, Spring, Hibernate / JPA, Primefaces, and unit tests (TDD, BDD), using JUnit with Mockito and testing automation with WebDriver + Selenium and Espresso for instrumented tests for native applications. My main interests are to build networking in the technology area to exchange experiences. Be very welcome and feel free to contact me.
